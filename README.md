# 💫 About Me:
Hey, I'm Steyn 👋<br><br>I'm a third-year BSc IT student at North-West University (NWU). For the past two years<br>I've worked as a lecturer assistant / demi at the university, helping younger students<br>get to grips with courses I did well in myself.<br><br>Game creation has been lingering in my head since I was a kid — it's a dream I've never<br>quite let go of, even on the days I don't have the energy to chase it. Lately I've been<br>drawn to the code running underneath most games, and C++ is where I feel most at home —<br>it's my favourite language to write in, and "build my own game one day" is still very<br>much the plan.<br><br>A few things about me outside of code: I really like dinosaurs (no shame), music, and<br>quiet, calm places. My favourite spot in the world is a small hidden house in the<br>mountains near my hometown in KZN, where a tire swing moves slowly between two massive<br>pine trees and the wind drifts through the needles.<br><br>Before university, I competed in target shooting for five years in high school — earned<br>national colours, ranked 1st in South Africa two years running, and was invited to<br>represent SA internationally, though I was never able to make the trip.<br><br>🦖 Currently building: small C++ experiments — pathfinding, generative patterns, and<br>recreations of classic games from scratch.
